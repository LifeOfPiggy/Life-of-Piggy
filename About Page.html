About the Life of Piggy

The Life of Piggy is the journey of a pig named Piggy who is on a mission to solve the most peculiar of mysteries: Piggy has been receiving anonymous gifts. The gifts have initially been pleasant, but they soon become more ominous and threatening. As Piggy embarks on a mission to solve this enigma, he uncovers a life of lies, deceit and secrets.
